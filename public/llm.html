Dosage & Usage

PopulationDose
Adults and adolescents 14+1–2 sachets per day, or as directed by a physician
MethodOpen sachet, place contents directly in mouth. Do not dissolve in water.
TimingAny time — food, tea, and coffee do not interfere with absorption

Contraindications

  • Hypersensitivity to iron salts, ascorbic acid (Vitamin C), or any form of Vitamin B₁₂
  • Hemosiderosis or hemochromatosis (iron overload disorders)
  • Anemias not related to iron deficiency
  • Hyperoxaluria
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding: consult a physician before use

Side Effects

Possible gastrointestinal disturbances (nausea, constipation, diarrhea). Due to the liposomal form, the frequency of side effects is significantly reduced compared to conventional iron preparations.

Storage

  • Temperature: below 30°C
  • Keep away from light, heat, and moisture
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Shelf life: 24 months from manufacture date
